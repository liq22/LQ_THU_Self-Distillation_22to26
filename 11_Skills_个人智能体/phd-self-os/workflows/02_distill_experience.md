# Workflow: Distill Experience

Use when converting scattered PhD experiences into durable lessons.

## Steps

1. Collect 3–7 related notes.
2. Identify the repeated pattern.
3. Separate context-specific details from reusable judgment.
4. Write in this structure:

```markdown
# 经验标题

## 一句话

## 发生在什么场景

## 当时的问题

## 我做过什么

## 后来证明有效/无效的地方

## 可以复用的判断标准

## 不适用的情况

## 给后来者的提醒

## 来源
```

## Quality bar

A good distilled lesson should be concrete enough that a junior researcher can act on it, but bounded enough that it does not pretend to be universal.
