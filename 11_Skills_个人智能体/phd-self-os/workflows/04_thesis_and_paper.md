# Workflow: Thesis and Paper Support

Use for paper summaries, rebuttal reflections, thesis chapters, defense materials, and publication narratives.

## Steps

1. Confirm the exact paper/project/chapter.
2. Retrieve from `03_Papers_论文与发表/` and `04_Thesis_毕业论文/` first.
3. Cross-check research facts in `02_Research_科研工作/`.
4. For claims about novelty, results, or limitations, require source support.
5. For reviewer or advisor comments, mark source type:
   - direct quote;
   - paraphrase;
   - user's interpretation.
6. Produce output in the requested form.

## Recommended output for paper history

```markdown
# 论文名：过程复盘

## 背景

## 核心问题

## 关键转折

## 投稿与反馈

## 我们如何修改

## 最后形成的贡献

## 这篇论文教会我的事

## 来源
```
